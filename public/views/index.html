Cartoset

  • It’s a new highly customizable geoportal solution based on open source technologies

  • For anybody that needs to publish and curate a geospatial dataset, in a simple and beautiful way

  • Because it is extremely flexible so that you can tell your own engaging story.

Features

A lot of features out of the box, and great support to design and develop your own stuff

  • GeoPortal uses Ruby On Rails, Refinery and PostGIS, a complete open-source framework, free to be used.

  • Support for points, polygons, multi polygons, lines, polilines, vectorial data layers...

  • GeoPortal supports multiple tile map vendors: Google Maps, OpenStreet Maps, CloudMade, Bing...

  • You can edit all geospatial data as you wish: point coordinates, drag-n-drop polygonal shape editor, associated metadata...

  • Carefully designed, GeoPortal’s backoffice is easy-to-use and highly efficient. Check these screenshots.

  • Absolutely all pages and elements in GeoPortal can be easily changed through custom CSSs.

  • Ready to cross your data with other API’s such Youtube, Panoramio and flickr in just one click

  • Keep the discussion in wikipedia, collaborate with them and avoid moderating content

    • OGC services Add-on

    • RSS and GeoRSS support

    • External WMS, WMC support
